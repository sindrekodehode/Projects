Bergen is surrounded by mountains and ocean. If you don't like either you could always get lost in some of the dense forest scattered around.

The climate is usually both wet and cold and luckily the mountains tend to funnel the wind so it can get extra windy as well. Nothing like a fresh ocean breeze to freeze you to your very core.

The people tend to be as open and welcoming as a clam. Once people get to know and trust you, they tend to be more open, but good luck getting people to know and trust you!
